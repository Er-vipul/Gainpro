About Us

We founded Gain.pro because we needed it ourselves​

In our previous careers, at McKinsey and Bridgepoint, we had access to all data providers – and often felt there were too many already. But to do our job we needed more transparency and insight than any existing product could offer. This meant tedious manual work for us and our teams. We love technology and saw the opportunity for a game-changing digital solution – Gain.pro! We save daily encrypted backups for 30 days to ensure your data is safe and secure, and store them redundantly across multiple availability zones. We use third-party monitoring services to track Gain.pro's availability and have engineers on-call to rapidly investigate and address any outages.
